4L trophy

Humanitarian raid - Aftermovie




4Lit'z Gaux! Liz and Margaux at the 4L Trophy 2017! After nearly 6000 km, we brought back our superb 4L! This journey gave us a lot of emotions as expected! In two weeks, we faced the roads of France, Spain and especially Morocco between mountains and desert, rain and tornado of sand, snow and great sun, so many landscapes inked in our memories that we are not ready to forget! But the 4L Trophy is not only that, it's also solidarity (especially in the famous sandboxes in which we spent many hours in the company of crews who helped us to the end), wonderful encounters with the desert children with whom we had the chance to spend a day filled with games and exchange as ambassadors thanks to the Desert Children's Association! Leaving at 4L, we did not realize that we were finally leaving, and now that we are back, we do not realize that it is already over ... Here is the video summary / nostalgic of our adventure!

  • Date: February 2017
  • Role: Realisator/Video editor
